Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026 — AI Sales Pulse, Inc.

AI Sales Pulse, Inc. ("we," "us," or "Pulse") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Pulse platform at app.aisalespulse.com.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, company name, phone number (optional), and password (stored as a salted hash — we never store plaintext passwords).

Business Data: Customer records, messages, follow-ups, sales data, and other information you enter into Pulse. This data belongs to your company.

Integration Data: If you connect third-party services (Outlook, WhatsApp, ERP systems), we access only the data necessary to provide our features. We do not sell or share this data.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics such as pages visited and feature usage to improve our product. We do not use third-party tracking scripts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Pulse platform
  • To send transactional emails (2FA codes, password resets, daily digests)
  • To improve our product based on aggregated, anonymous usage patterns
  • To provide customer support

We never sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in encrypted PostgreSQL databases hosted on secure cloud infrastructure. All data in transit is protected with TLS 1.3 encryption. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We support two-factor authentication for additional account security.

4. Multi-Tenant Data Isolation

Pulse is built with strict multi-tenant isolation. Each company's data is logically separated at the database level. No company can access another company's records, messages, customers, or settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Resend — for transactional email delivery
  • Anthropic Claude — for AI-powered features (your data is not used to train AI models)
  • Railway — for cloud hosting infrastructure

6. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your business data

To exercise these rights, email hello@aisalespulse.com.

7. Cookies

Pulse uses only essential cookies required for authentication (JWT session tokens stored in localStorage). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or an in-app announcement.
